About Me

Energy analyst exploring the intersection of markets, data, and clean energy

I'm Vasudev, an energy analyst based in Pune, India.

My work sits at the intersection of battery storage and US wholesale energy markets — mainly ERCOT, PJM, CAISO, and MISO. I spend most of my time thinking about how batteries make money: dispatch optimization, revenue stacks, market design, and how all of it is shifting as storage capacity scales up.

I started The Merit Order because writing helps me think more clearly. The posts here are how I process what I'm seeing in the markets — sometimes data-heavy, sometimes just an observation that felt worth putting into words.

Five years in, I'm still learning. The markets keep moving.
