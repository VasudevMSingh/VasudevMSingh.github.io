About Me

Energy analyst exploring the intersection of markets, data, and clean energy

Introduction

Hey there! Welcome to my digital home - a space where I share my professional journey, personal projects, and evolving perspectives on the world. Think of this as a curated collection of everything that makes me tick, from energy market analysis to basketball statistics, and everything in between.

Professional Profile

I'm Vasudev, an energy analyst and project manager based out of Pune. I have 4+ years of experience in Energy Markets research and analysis, out of which 3 years have been at a dynamic and rapidly growing startup.

Currently, I'm diving deep into US wholesale energy markets and leading the product development and marketing efforts for my organization, where most of my days are spent optimizing utility-scale energy storage plants, researching battery assets development and performances, and developing a branding and marketing strategy to bring visibility to the awesome work that we do.

What really gets me going is the challenge of deconstructing complex energy market data and turning it into actionable insights. Over the past three years, I've developed expertise in:

  • Finding opportunities hidden in market data
  • Optimizing battery storage operations for maximum value
  • Translating complex market trends into winning strategies
  • Building tools that make sense of market chaos

Education

My journey took an interesting turn at Arizona State University, where I pursued my Master's in Electrical Power and Energy. It wasn't just about the degree - it was about building a foundation in:

  • The intricate dance of energy markets
  • The backbone of power systems
  • The tools to turn data into decisions

The global pandemic threw a curveball during my time at ASU, but it also taught me valuable lessons about adaptability and resilience. I completed my degree and returned to India, armed with knowledge and ready to take on new challenges in a familiar yet different context.
