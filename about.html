> PROFESSIONAL_PROFILE

I'm Vasudev an energy analyst and project manager based out of Pune. I have 4+ years of experience in Energy Markets research and analysis, out of which 3 years have been at a dynamic and rapidly growing startup. Currently, I'm diving deep into US wholesale energy markets and leading the product development and marketing efforts for my organization, where most of my days are spent optimizing utility-scale energy storage plants, researching battery assets development and performances, and developing a branding and marketing strategy to bring visibility to the awesome work that we do.

What really gets me going is the challenge of deconstructing complex energy market data and turning it into actionable insights. Over the past three years, I've developed a knack for:

  • Translating complex market trends into winning strategies
  • Building tools that make sense of market chaos
